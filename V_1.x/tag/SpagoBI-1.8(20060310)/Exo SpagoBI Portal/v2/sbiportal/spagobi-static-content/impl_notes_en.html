Implementation note

Portlet

Portlets are autonomous and independent application windows. They are freely usable inside portal contexts, supporting the JSR 168 specification, by means of a simple configuration and without development needs.
SpagoBI releases different portlets according to the different user's typologies (administrator, developer, tester, end-user). You can insert these portlets into your corporate portal or into a particular Business Intelligence environment.
This demo is organized in a very simple way, in order to keep your attention on the SpagoBI's functional aspects. So, the portlets fill whole pages, but you can form freely their layout by inserting other different portlets, according to your particular scenario.

Independence from the database and the operating system

Now, some RDBMS scripts are available, which enables you to use SpagoBI in different platform both free/open source software, than proprietary software:
  • PostgreSQL
  • MySql
  • Oracle
  • DB2 (soon available).
In any case, there is a data access abstraction layer (Hibernate) that enable the applicative to be independent from the specific RDBMS.
SpagoBI is an application software Java-based; you can use it on different operating systems.
