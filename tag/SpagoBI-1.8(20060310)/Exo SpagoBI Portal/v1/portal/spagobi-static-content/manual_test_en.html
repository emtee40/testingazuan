Tester

The tester takes the responsibility to verify the formal correctness of the registered documents and if they fulfil the requirements. He can access to a portlet which allows him to execute his documents that are in a test state, by means of the functional tree-view.

The tester can execute a document by simulating all its predefined roles. When the test has been succeeded, he can change the documents’ state to production. In this way the document is available for the end user.
